Duolingo — Proposal Process

This page shows how I went from a blank page to a structured proposal and prototype: outlining, research synthesis, iteration, and final presentation.

View Final Writeup → View Prototype →

Workflow

  1. Structured outline first: Started from a PRD-style checklist and created a clear section plan before writing.
  2. Research + notes: Gathered market and competitor context, then drafted early ideas directly in the document.
  3. Iteration: Revised for clarity and stronger reasoning while keeping intermediate versions to track improvements.
  4. Prototype pass: Translated the core concept into a first-time Figma prototype flow.
  5. Finalize: Tightened the narrative into a concise proposal with clear features and success metrics.
