Duolingo — Sample Feature Proposal

A sample product proposal for Duolingo focused on improving conversational fluency through peer interaction and community-driven engagement.

This project is an independent product proposal created for an application. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Duolingo.

Market Overview

The global language-learning market was valued at approximately $20B in 2023 and is projected to exceed $90B by 2030. Rapid advances in AI tutoring, voice recognition, and adaptive learning are transforming how learners practice languages. Despite this, users struggle with repetitive exercises, limited feedback specificity, and lack of authentic dialogue. This creates opportunity for more human-centered, conversational features.

Competitor Analysis

Babbel
  • Structured lessons with cultural context
  • Adult professional audience
  • Paid-only model
  • Strong conversational realism, limited gamification
HelloTalk
  • Peer-to-peer native speaker exchange
  • No structured curriculum
  • Experience depends on partner reliability
  • Strong immersion, weak quality control

User Personas

Emma (36)

Nurse learning Spanish to better communicate with patients. Pain points: generic feedback, limited real-life dialogue, lack of cultural nuance. Likely paid user.

Nate (19)

Student preparing for study abroad in China. Pain points: anxiety about live conversation, repetitive drills, weak tone feedback. Free-tier user who values gamification.

Proposed Features

1. Language Match (Swipe-Based Peer Learning)

Pairs learners with native speakers through a swipe interface matching interests, fluency, and goals. Builds conversational confidence through real dialogue.

2. Peer Feedback & Rewards

Users rate sessions and earn badges or leaderboard points. Encourages accountability and safe community interaction.

3. Open Opportunities Board

Users post or join language exchanges, cultural calls, or speaking challenges. Extends Duolingo beyond structured lessons.

Key Metrics

  • Peer Conversation Rate — Measures adoption of new feature.
  • 30-Day Retention — Tracks sustained engagement.
  • Average Session Length — Indicates learning depth and enjoyment.
