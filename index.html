Hi, I’m Giancarla!

I design and build human-centered technical systems at the intersection of engineering and product. My work spans computer architecture, embedded systems, product strategy, and UI/UX design — translating complex systems into usable, thoughtful experiences.

Explore My Work

Circuits

Product Management

Technical product strategy, user research, cross-functional execution, and systems thinking. Aligning complex systems with human needs and business outcomes

UI/UX Design

Personal Projects

On Iteration & Growth

Design is iterative by nature. While the work showcased here reflects my thinking and process at different stages, I’m constantly refining how I approach clarity, usability, and system behavior.

If you notice areas that could be improved, whether it be interaction details, visual hierarchy, user flows, or edge cases, I would genuinely love to talk about them. Thoughtful critique is one of the most valuable parts of growing as a designer and engineer.

I’m always open to conversations about what works, what doesn’t, and how systems can be made more intuitive and human-centered.
